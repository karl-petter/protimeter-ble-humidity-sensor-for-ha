NOTES:
------
- Devices: 2× Protimeter BLE Humidity Sensor (installed in cellar)
- Target Platform: Home Assistant (custom_components)
- BLE library: bleak + bleak-retry-connector (via HA bluetooth integration)
- Protocol source: ProtimeterApp.dll (Xamarin/.NET, decompiled with monodis)
- Data format: fully reverse-engineered and verified against captured data
- Design: history-based (device records on its own schedule; HA reads
  history periodically — default weekly — to preserve battery life)
- BT connectivity: requires ESPHome proxy with active: true near sensors
  (Shelly BT gateway is passive-only and cannot establish connections)
